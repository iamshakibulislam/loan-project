Questions thoughtful founders ask.

Straight answers. No jargon. No runaround.

What exactly is Atlas Fund Capital? ▼
We are a private capital advisory firm that connects established businesses with a carefully curated consortium of institutional funding sources. We are not a bank. We operate as your strategic intermediary — identifying, negotiating, and securing the most advantageous terms across our network on your behalf.
How does the process actually work? ▼
You begin by completing the qualification form above — it takes roughly ninety seconds. A senior funding advisor reviews your profile, identifies the institutions in our consortium best positioned to serve your needs, and presents curated options. You choose the path that fits. Most clients receive capital within one to three business days of final approval.
What do I need to qualify? ▼
Our consortium members maintain varying criteria, but the baseline typically includes: a minimum of six months of operating history, monthly revenue exceeding $10,000, and a personal credit score above 500. If your profile falls outside these parameters, we can often identify alternative pathways.
Does this inquiry affect my credit? ▼
No. Our initial assessment uses a soft inquiry that leaves no trace on your credit report. A formal credit pull occurs only if you elect to proceed with a specific financing offer, and only after you've reviewed and accepted the preliminary terms.
How quickly can I access capital? ▼
Revenue-based facilities and lines of credit can fund within the same business day. Structured term products typically settle within 24 to 72 hours. SBA-backed programs follow a longer underwriting timeline — generally two to four weeks — but deliver the most favorable long-term economics.
