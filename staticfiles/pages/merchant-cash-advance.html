Revenue-Based Financing

Capital secured against your future receivables. Repayments flex with your sales — you pay more when business surges and less during slower cycles. Funding as soon as 24 hours.

Apply Now →

Pre-qualification has no impact on your credit profile

Capital that breathes with your business.

Revenue-based financing delivers an upfront capital infusion in exchange for an agreed-upon share of your daily card receivables. There is no fixed monthly payment, no rigid amortization schedule, and no penalty for seasonal fluctuation. Your remittance rises when revenue climbs and adjusts downward when it contracts.

This structure makes it uniquely suited for businesses with strong card-processing volumes — restaurants, retail storefronts, hospitality operators, and service-based enterprises. You receive the capital you need without forcing your cash flow into a calendar it doesn't follow.

  • ✓ Access from $5,000 to $20 million
  • ✓ Repayment calibrated to daily card receipts
  • ✓ No fixed term or prepayment penalty
