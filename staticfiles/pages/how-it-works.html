The Process

How Atlas Fund Capital Works

Three steps from qualification to capital. One advisor orchestrating the entire journey.

Begin Your Qualification →
90 secs
Initial qualification
Dedicated
Advisor assigned
As soon as 24 hrs
Capital deployment
1

Tell us about your business.

Complete the qualification form — it takes roughly ninety seconds. We'll ask what you need, how you generate revenue, and a few details about your operating history. No documents. No tax returns. No commitment.

2

Review curated options.

A senior advisor who specializes in your industry reviews your profile and identifies the capital sources best positioned to serve you. You receive a concise comparison of available terms — rates, structures, timelines — with plain-English explanations of each.

3

Capital deployed.

Once you select a path, your advisor manages the remaining process — final documentation, verification, and settlement. Revenue-based facilities and lines of credit can fund within the same business day. Structured products typically settle within 24 to 72 hours.

Why founders choose this approach.

The advantages of working through a capital advisor rather than applying institution by institution.

🔒

One inquiry. One footprint.

Your profile is evaluated once through a soft inquiry. You see offers from multiple sources without each institution pulling your credit independently.

⚡

Speed through curation.

We know which institutions are actively deploying in which sectors and at what terms. We match you directly, eliminating dead-end applications.

🎯

Terms negotiated on your behalf.

Your advisor presents options and helps you evaluate them — not as a salesperson, but as someone whose compensation depends on your acceptance, not the institution's.

Ready to begin?

Ninety seconds to discover your options. No credit impact. No obligation to proceed.
