How the partner program works.

You refer businesses to Atlas Fund Capital using your unique partner link. When someone submits a lead through your link — and that business is earning over $25,000 per month, has been operating for at least one year, and has a credit score of 500 or above — you earn $100. No caps. No quotas. No waiting for the deal to close.

For partners who prefer a larger upside, we also offer a pay-per-close model: 2% of the total deal size when a business you introduced closes a financing facility. Submit deals directly from your partner dashboard — our team handles the rest.
