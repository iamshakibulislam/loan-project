You've already earned the revenue. Access it now.

Invoice factoring — also known as receivables financing — converts your outstanding B2B or B2G invoices into immediate working capital. You sell the receivable at a modest discount in exchange for the bulk of its value today, rather than waiting for your client's payment cycle to complete.

This instrument is particularly powerful for enterprises whose business model involves extended payment terms: staffing firms that pay weekly but bill net-60, manufacturers funding raw materials months before finished goods ship, wholesalers bridging the gap between procurement and fulfillment.

How receivables financing works.

Three steps from invoice to available capital.

1

Submit your receivables

2

3
